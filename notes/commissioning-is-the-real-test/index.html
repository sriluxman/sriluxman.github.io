Commissioning is the real test

Architecture becomes honest when it meets a machine, a deadline, and incomplete information.

February 19, 2026

The cleanest architecture diagram is still a hypothesis.

Commissioning is where the hypothesis meets a real machine, a real customer, and the part nobody documented because everybody assumed it was obvious.

That is not an edge case. That is the system becoming honest.
