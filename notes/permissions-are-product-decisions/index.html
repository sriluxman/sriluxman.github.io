Permissions are product decisions

Agent autonomy is defined less by prompts than by what tools are allowed to do.

March 7, 2026

When an agent can only suggest, the product is a reviewer.

When it can create a branch, open an issue, or operate a machine workflow, the product becomes something else.

Permissions are not infrastructure plumbing. They define the experience.
