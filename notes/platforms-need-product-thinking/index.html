Platforms need product thinking

Internal does not mean the users have no choice.

January 29, 2026

An internal platform still competes.

It competes with scripts, workarounds, old habits, and the colleague who knows how to make the legacy system behave.

Calling something mandatory does not remove that competition. It only hides the adoption problem.
