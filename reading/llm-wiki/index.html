LLM wiki

A simple pattern for letting an LLM maintain a durable knowledge artifact from raw sources.

Open source

The useful insight is that the wiki is a compiled artifact, not a bag of retrieved chunks.

Raw sources remain raw. The model maintains a readable structure, an index, and a log. That simplicity creates a strong boundary for agentic work.
